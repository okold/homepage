About Me

My name is Olga, and I'm a student living in Calgary, Alberta, Canada studying Computer Science at Mount Royal University. I spent a semester at the Alberta University of the Arts (back when it was still ACAD) and I still remember one thing I was told in drawing class: some of the best artists in history were also the best mathematicians. Now, I've never considered myself much of a math person, but I've discovered that computer repair is my craft and programming is my art. For a look at my work, feel free to take a look at my GitHub.

I also like to write the occassional short story, a collection of which you can find here.
