About Me

My name is Olga, and I'm a student living in Calgary, Alberta, studying Computer Science at Mount Royal University.

Before finding my passion in technology, I spent a semester at the Alberta University of the Arts (back when it was still ACAD). I most fondly remember drawing class, where we learned to calculate depth and light, and one thing the teacher said had stuck: some of the best artists in history were also the best mathematicians. I didn't end up drawing for a living, but I still discovered art in bits, logic, and the flow of words on a screen.

I like to write the occassional short story, which can be found here.
